POLICY

Overview

At Limitless Personal Coaching LLC, we strive to provide exceptional online fitness coaching services tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Our fulfillment policy outlines the terms and conditions regarding the delivery of our services, ensuring a clear understanding between the company and our clients.

Service Delivery

  1. Initial Consultation

    Upon purchasing any of our fitness coaching packages, clients will receive an email confirmation with a link to schedule their initial consultation. The initial consultation must be scheduled within 14 days of purchase.

  2. Customized Fitness Plans

    Following the initial consultation, our fitness coach will develop a customized fitness plan tailored to the client’s goals, preferences, and fitness level. The customized plan will be delivered within 7 business days after the initial consultation.

  3. Ongoing Coaching Sessions

    Coaching sessions will be conducted via video call (e.g., Zoom, Skype) at times agreed upon between the client and the coach. Clients will receive a link to book their ongoing coaching sessions in advance.

  4. Progress Tracking and Adjustments

    Clients will have access to progress tracking tools and regular check-ins to monitor their progress. Adjustments to the fitness plan will be made as necessary based on the client’s progress and feedback.

Payment and Refunds

  1. Payment Terms

    Payment for fitness coaching packages is required upfront. We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods as indicated on our website.

  2. Refund Policy

    We appreciate your investment in our limitless personal coaching services. As we are committed to delivering the highest quality of coaching and support to our clients, we do not offer refunds. If you have any questions or concerns about your coaching experience, please don't hesitate to reach out through the contact section of this website. We are here to ensure that you have a positive and fulfilling coaching journey with us.

Cancellations and Rescheduling

  1. Session Cancellations

    Clients may cancel or reschedule a coaching session with at least 24 hours’ notice. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours’ notice will be considered a no-show and the session will be forfeited.

  2. Company-Initiated Cancellations

    In the rare event that a coach needs to cancel or reschedule a session, the client will be notified as soon as possible, and a makeup session will be arranged at no additional cost.

Client Responsibilities

  1. Timely Communication

    Clients are responsible for timely communication with their coach, including responding to emails and attending scheduled sessions.

  2. Adherence to the Plan

    Clients are expected to follow the fitness plan and provide honest feedback to ensure the plan remains effective and relevant.

Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding our fulfillment policy, please contact our customer service team at the Contact Section of this website. We are here to assist you and ensure a positive experience with our online fitness coaching services.

Privacy Policy

At Limitless Personal Coaching LLC, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our online fitness coaching services.

  1. Personal Information

    Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
    Demographic Information: Age, gender, and other relevant details.
    Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, and payment history.

  2. Health and Fitness Information

    Health History: Information related to your medical history, current health status, and any relevant conditions.
    Fitness Data: Workout preferences, fitness goals, activity levels, and progress tracking data.

  3. Usage Information
