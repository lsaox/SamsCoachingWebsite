PCI Compliance and Payment Security

At Limitless Personal Coaching LLC, we prioritize the security and privacy of our customers' payment information. We are committed to complying with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS), which sets forth stringent requirements for organizations that handle cardholder information.

What does PCI compliance mean for you as a customer of Limitless Personal Coaching LLC? It means that we adhere to industry best practices to ensure that your payment data is handled securely and responsibly. Our integration with Stripe, a leading payment processing platform, is designed with integration security in mind. Stripe's robust infrastructure and adherence to PCI standards provide an additional layer of security for your transactions.

Here's what we do to ensure the safety of your payment information:

  • Secure Website: Our website, including our payment form, is protected by HTTPS encryption and follows security best practices to safeguard your data during transmission.
  • PCI Compliance: We maintain PCI compliance, ensuring that our systems and processes meet the stringent security requirements outlined by the PCI DSS.
  • Ongoing Monitoring: We continuously monitor our systems for any signs of suspicious activity and regularly update our security measures to stay ahead of emerging threats.
  • Anti-Fraud Measures: We employ advanced fraud detection tools and techniques to detect and prevent fraudulent transactions, ensuring that your payment information is handled safely and securely.

By choosing Limitless Personal Coaching LLC and utilizing our integration with Stripe, you can trust that your payment information is in good hands, and your privacy and security are our top priorities. If you have any questions or concerns about our payment security practices, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Overview

At Limitless Personal Coaching LLC, we strive to provide exceptional online fitness coaching services tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Our fulfillment policy outlines the terms and conditions regarding the delivery of our services, ensuring a clear understanding between the company and our clients.

Service Delivery

  1. Initial Consultation

    Upon purchasing any of our fitness coaching packages, clients will receive an email confirmation with a link to schedule their initial consultation. The initial consultation must be scheduled within 14 days of purchase.

  2. Customized Fitness Plans

    Following the initial consultation, our fitness coach will develop a customized fitness plan tailored to the client’s goals, preferences, and fitness level. The customized plan will be delivered within 7 business days after the initial consultation.

  3. Ongoing Coaching Sessions

    Coaching sessions will be conducted via video call (e.g., Zoom, Skype) at times agreed upon between the client and the coach. Clients will receive a link to book their ongoing coaching sessions in advance.

  4. Progress Tracking and Adjustments

    Clients will have access to progress tracking tools and regular check-ins to monitor their progress. Adjustments to the fitness plan will be made as necessary based on the client’s progress and feedback.
